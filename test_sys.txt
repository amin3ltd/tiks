1. Organizer creates or verifies check-in list
2. Open check-in page/app if available
3. Search attendee/order

1. Try checkout with missing required fields
2. Try invalid email
3. Try buying more tickets than quota

1. Verify pages load without server errors
2. Verify no obvious 500/403 unexpected pages
3. Verify main customer flow works on mobile viewport
